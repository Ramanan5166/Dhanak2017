Biped Robot

An Arduino based Robotics Workshop By Robokart

The workshop teaches you the fundamentals of building autonomous robots by integration with Atmega328 microcontroller. The workshop focuses on conceptualization and designing of complex systems and will help clear concepts related to Embedded systems in simple manner. This workshop teaches to interface SG90 Servo motors with mechanism of walking. Students will learn to control servo motors and to achieve movements of Robotic legs. This workshop is a technical practice to build a walking Robot using ATmega328 microcontroller along with servo mechanism. This workshop gives practical touch about robotics and mechanism and software knowledge for robotics.
